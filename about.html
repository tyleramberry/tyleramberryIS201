About Me

I'm Tyler, a BYU freshman, founder, and AI builder living in Utah Valley.

I'm Pre-Business at Brigham Young University with an intended Strategic Management major, exploring consulting through BYU's Finance Society and Management Consulting Association.

Outside of class, I own and run Elite Lawn Care, a yard-care company I founded in 2019, and consult through Edge AI Solutions, where I help small businesses adopt practical AI tooling.

Before BYU I spent two years as a full-time volunteer representative in Tahiti, French Polynesia, where I picked up French and Tahitian and learned more about leading people than any classroom could teach.
