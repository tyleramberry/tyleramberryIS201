What we do

Edge AI Solutions helps small and mid-sized businesses adopt AI in three core ways:

  1. AI Agents
    • 24/7 lead-response chatbots for service businesses
    • Internal Q&A copilots that read your company docs
    • Sales-qualifying agents that book straight to your calendar
  2. Workflow Automation
    • Auto-routing customer inquiries to the right team member
    • AI-summarized inboxes and CRM updates
    • Recurring report generation and delivery
  3. Strategy & Training
    • 1-hour workshops for your team on practical AI use
    • Custom prompt libraries tailored to your workflows
    • Ongoing advisory as your AI stack grows

Our process

1. Discover

We map your workflows and find the highest-leverage spots for AI to step in.

2. Build

We design and ship working agents or automations in days, not months.

3. Train

Your team learns to use, tweak, and maintain everything we build.

4. Iterate

We monitor results and improve agents over time as your business changes.
